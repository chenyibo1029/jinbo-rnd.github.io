Biography

I am currently a Ph.D. candidate in Department of Management Sciences at City University of Hong Kong.

I received my Bachelor of Engineering degree in Computer Science & Technology from Zhejiang University of Technology. I was an ACM-ICPC player and won a gold medal in the 2009 Asia Harbin Regional Contest.

Research and Career Planning

I research in the areas of discrete optimization, logistics and Innovative Applications of O.R.. I am also interested in analysis of logistics, information processing of Chinese texts, web development, and algorithm design.

I plan to get my Ph.D. in 2015, and both academic and industrial jobs are in my consideration.
